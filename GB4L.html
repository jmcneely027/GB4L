Green Beret For Life

We're here to help share information and develop a network after our time on a team is up. We help facilitate the network connections as well as opportunities as you approach the end of your chapter.

Known Opportunities

As we approach the end of our SF career, we're always told that if were a medic, we should go to PA school. If were an echo, we should work in tech. We're here to break that line of thinking. Whatever your background, whatever your experience, we're here to help offer other options to help you be successful in the future.

Continued Network

Being on an ODA provided you with an exemplary brotherhood and support system. After leaving the service, it's an unfortunate realization that teams like that arent nearly as strong. Our goal is to continue the support and brotherhood after you get out. We aim to maintain contact with individuals to keep the network strong after youve left the team.
